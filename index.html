The Mabande of Liberia
History

The Mabande family is a quintessential example of resilience and unity, embodying values that span across village in East Africa,the Mabandes have navigated the complexities of both rural and urban life,adapting to changes with remarkable fortitude. The patriarch, juma Mabande, started his life as a farmer, toiling the land to provide for his family. His hard work and dedication laid a strong foundation for the family's future. His wife, Amina, played an equally pivotal role,managing the household and instilling in their children the importance of education and community.
